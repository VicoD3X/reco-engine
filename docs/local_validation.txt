- [a1..a5] is the ordered list of recommended article IDs.

Interpretation:

- Users 0, 1 and 2 exist in the click history. The system generates personalized recommendations from articles previously viewed by each user.
- User 999999 does not exist in the sample data. The system falls back to globally popular articles.

Validated behavior:

- The engine returns 5 recommendations.
- Personalization works when history is available.
- The cold-start fallback keeps the system robust when no user history exists.
